Feed Planner

Feed planning is one of the features this Instagram management tool was started with. Ghost Follower Reports

Ghost followers can be a huge problem for Influencers, especially those really striving to maintain an authentic reach and following. Ghost followers are accounts that follow you however don’t engage whatsoever with your content. Melon Stats has a feature to help you identify and remove these followers from your account, keeping your following clean and engaged. While this isn’t the first Instagram management tool to provide this service, having it as an all-in-one solution with all the other features makes managing your account that much easier.

 

Growth Analysis

The other feature that began Melon Stats is growth analysis. The Melon Stats team it was absolutely key to have a wide range of account statistics when it comes to creating an Instagram management tool. With Melon Stats you can track daily growth on followers, media and engagement to see how your strategy is performing. Repost Finder

We haven’t found an Instagram management tool to provide solutions for this, that is until now. With Melon Stats you can find accounts based on your location and niche that are likely to repost your content to help grow your following.

 

Competitor Tracking

In addition to monitoring your own account, Melon Stats allows you to add an additional 4 accounts and get reports on their activity. Account Statistics

Available to all Wolf Global members is Account Statistics, which accessible with the /stats command and provides basic information on your such as follower count and engagement ratios. Media Report

Hashtag Entry Report

Post tracking is the final report accessible by Wolf Global Analytics and provides members with reports on the account’s most recent post for the first 30 minutes of it going live. Once this report is activated with the /tracking command, the member will receive an engagement progress report every 5 minutes detailing likes and comments received. This report is great to help you see how people engage with your content as it goes live and determine when are the busiest times for your content overall.
